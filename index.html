Scales of Destiny Working Title

An ARPG that features a small dragon protagonist. The game is designed to be accessible for casual players while still offering something for more hardcore gamers. Players will embark on an adventure filled with quests, battles, and discoveries, as they develop their dragon's skills and abilities while advancing their own personalized storyline. The game also includes customization options for the dragon's appearance and gear, as well as the opportunity to build relationships with other characters in the game world.

Tap Tap Zoo

A mobile game that lets players create their own virtual menagerie by collecting adorable and colorful animals. The game features relaxed and idle gameplay mechanics, where players earn hearts by tapping on the screen to find new animals. With each new animal added, the menagerie grows, and players can watch as the animals roam freely in their virtual space. The game's focus is on collecting and leveling up the animals. Offers a fun and casual experience for players who enjoy collecting and interacting with cute and colorful animals. Mini-games too! I learned a lot from this project.

Shotz

This one is a mobile arcade game where players shoot a ball through increasingly challenging sets of obstacles. The objective is to navigate through the obstacles with proper timing and earn points. This project allowed me to experiment with different VFX techniques, and players can unlock different ball types and trails as rewards for their progress.
