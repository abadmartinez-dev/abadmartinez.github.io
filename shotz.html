Shotz

A simple, yet challenging arcade game that puts your timing skills to the test.

Simple and Clean

As I worked on this project, I had the opportunity to expand my skillset in various areas, including UI/UX design, visual effects, and creating custom editor tools to streamline the development process.

I also gained experience in audio integration and design while working on Shotz. I created all of the sound effects myself, ensuring that they were cohesive with the overall gameplay experience. Through this process, I learned the importance of audio design in creating an immersive and engaging game.

Additionally, I experimented with basic procedural levels, which allowed for a greater level of replayability and variation in the game. This experience has given me a deeper understanding of the various components that go into creating a successful game and helped me to further refine my skills as a developer.
